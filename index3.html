Histroy of Mercedes Benze

In 1901 the name of "mercedes" was registered by Daiier-Motoren-Gesellschaft (DMG) worldwide as a protected trademark Mercedes-Benz traces its origins to Karl Benz's creation of the first petrol-powered car The Mercedes automobile was first marketed in 1901 by Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft Emil Jellinek, a European automobile entrepreneur who worked with DMG, created the trademark in 1902, naming the 1901 Mercedes 35 hp after his daughter Mercedes Jellinek.Subsidiaries and alliances As part of the Daimler AG company, the Mercedes-Benz Cars division includes Mercedes-Benz and Smart car production. Mercedes-AMG became a majority owned division of Mercedes-Benz in 1999. The company was integrated into DaimlerChrysler in 1999,[18] and became Mercedes-Benz AMG beginning on 1 January 1999.
